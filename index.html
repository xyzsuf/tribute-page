Tribute to SRK

an Indian actor, film producer, and television personality who works in Hindi films.

Shah Rukh Khan (pronounced [ˈʃɑːɦɾʊx xɑːn]; born 2 November 1965), also known by the initialism SRK, is an Indian actor.
Referred to in the media as the "Baadshah of Bollywood", "King of Bollywood" and "King Khan", he has appeared in more than 80 films.
He has been awarded the Padma Shri by the Government of India. Khan has been described as one of the most successful film stars in the world.
